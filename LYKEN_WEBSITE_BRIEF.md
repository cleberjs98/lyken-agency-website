# Lyken Agency Website — Creative & Visual Direction Brief

## 1. Purpose of This Document

This document defines the creative, visual, strategic, and experiential direction for the **Lyken Agency website**.

It should be placed in the root of the project and used as a reference for development, design decisions, copywriting, layout structure, animation behavior, bilingual content, and quality auditing.

The goal is to ensure that the website does not become a generic agency landing page, but a premium, technological, interactive brand experience that communicates what Lyken Agency truly is.

---

## 2. Core Website Concept

### Experience Name

**From Idea to Living Brand**

### Portuguese Version

**Da Ideia à Marca Viva**

### Central Idea

The Lyken Agency website should communicate the journey of a brand being born, structured, transformed, and expanded into the digital world.

The experience should feel like:

> An idea being born inside an intelligent, premium, digital system.

The site should not feel static. It should feel alive, refined, strategic, and interactive.

The visual narrative should follow this transformation:

**Empty space → Spark → Structure → Identity → Digital Presence → Intelligence → Growth**

---

## 3. Brand Positioning for the Website

Lyken Agency should be presented as a premium creative and strategic agency that combines:

- Branding
- Brand strategy
- Visual identity

- This is premium.

## 7. Color Application Rules

The main combination should be:

**Deep green background + champagne gold details + subtle digital mesh**

Avoid:

- Excessive gold
- Pure black as the dominant background
- Bright neon colors
- Purple/pink cyberpunk gradients
- Generic blue tech themes
- Flat white layouts as the main experience

Gold should feel like a premium detail, not decoration everywhere.

## 13. Loading Experience

The loading screen should not be a generic spinner.

It should be the first brand experience.

### Concept

The loading animation represents the birth of an idea and its transformation into a living digital brand.

### Sequence

#### 1. Empty Premium Space

A deep green background appears with subtle texture.

The screen feels quiet, refined, and intentional.

#### 2. First Spark

A small champagne-gold point of light appears at the center.

This point represents the idea.

#### 3. Connection

Additional points appear.

Thin lines begin to connect them.

This represents structure and strategy.

#### 4. Living Mesh

The points and lines become a subtle digital mesh or wave.

This represents technology, intelligence, and digital transformation.

#### 5. Monogram Reveal

The LK monogram appears in metallic gold.

It should feel revealed by light, not abruptly inserted.

#### 6. Experience Phrase

The phrase appears:

**From idea to living brand.**

Portuguese:

**Da ideia à marca viva.**

#### 7. Transition to Hero

The mesh expands and becomes part of the Hero background.

The loading screen should transform into the website, not simply disappear.

### Loading Duration

Ideal duration:

**1.5 to 3 seconds**

It should be beautiful, but never annoying.

- Line drawing effects
- Subtle particle movement
- Slow mesh motion

## 28. Animations to Avoid

Avoid:

- Bounce effects
- Fast chaotic movement
- Overly strong neon glow
- Excessive parallax
- Game-like animations
- Random rotating objects
- Too many moving elements at once
- Long loading animations
- Generic spinner loading
- Cyberpunk clichés

The movement must feel alive, but controlled.
